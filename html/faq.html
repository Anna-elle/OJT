Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for the OJT program?

The program is open to unemployed Trinidad and Tobago citizens between 18-60 years old who are seeking job training opportunities.

How long does the training last?

The OJT training typically lasts between 3-6 months, depending on the specific program and industry.

Is there any cost to participate?

No, the OJT program is completely free for participants. In fact, trainees receive a stipend during their training period.

How do I apply?

You can apply online through our registration form or visit any Ministry of Labour office for assistance with the application process.

What happens after I complete the program?

Our team provides job placement assistance, and many participants are hired by their training employers. We also offer follow-up support for 6 months after completion.

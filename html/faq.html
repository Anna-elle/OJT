Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Who can apply for the OJT Program?
A: Any unemployed, eligible citizen of Trinidad and Tobago aged 18–60.
Q: How long does the training last?
A: Between 3 to 6 months, depending on the industry and employer requirements.
Q: Do I get paid during the training?
A: No direct salary, but some employers may offer allowances or transport assistance.
Q: Will I definitely get a job after the program?
A: While not guaranteed, many employers hire trainees upon successful completion.
Q: Can I choose the company I want to train with?
A: Preference is considered, but final placements are made based on availability and suitability.

Our Mission

The Ministry of Labour develops national labour policy, enforces labour legislation, and promotes fair labour practices across all industries in Trinidad and Tobago.

OJT Program Background

Our flagship On-the-Job Training (OJT) programme provides unemployed nationals with valuable work experience through agreements with local employers. The program supports workforce development, reduces unemployment, and helps participants transition into full-time employment.

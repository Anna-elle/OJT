About the Ministry of Labour

The Ministry of Labour is responsible for shaping and enforcing labor policies across Trinidad and Tobago. Its mission is to promote fair labor practices, improve workforce development, and empower citizens through various training programs like the On-the-Job Training (OJT) initiative.

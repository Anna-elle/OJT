OJT Program Overview

The On-the-Job Training (OJT) Program is a government-sponsored initiative designed to help unemployed individuals gain hands-on experience in real working environments. The program runs for up to six months and provides trainees with mentorship, on-site learning, and the opportunity to be hired permanently by participating employers.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Unemployed citizen of Trinidad and Tobago
  • Aged between 18 and 60 years
  • Completed at least Secondary School Level education

How It Works

  1. Register online or visit the nearest Ministry office
  2. Submit required documents
  3. Attend orientation and assessment session
  4. Get matched with a suitable employer
  5. Begin your 3–6 month training period
  6. Upon successful completion, apply for permanent position

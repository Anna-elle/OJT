Program Structure

The OJT training is typically three to six months in duration and includes:

  • Practical, hands-on work experience
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals
  • Regular progress evaluations
  • Post-training follow-up for job placement

Eligibility Requirements

To participate in the OJT program, applicants must:

  • Be a citizen of Trinidad and Tobago
  • Be between 18-60 years old
  • Be currently unemployed
  • Have a willingness to learn and work

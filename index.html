What is OJT?

The On-the-Job Training (OJT) program is an initiative administered by the Ministry of Labour to provide practical work experience for unemployed individuals. With the support of local employers, participants gain valuable skills to transition into full-time employment.

Benefits of OJT

  • Hands-on work experience
  • Skill development opportunities
  • Potential for permanent placement
  • Support from Ministry advisors
